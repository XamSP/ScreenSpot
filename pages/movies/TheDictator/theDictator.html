2012 1h 24m

The Dictator" is a satirical comedy film that follows the outrageous misadventures of General Aladeen, a ruthless and eccentric dictator of the fictional country Wadiya. When Aladeen's rule is threatened by a democratic uprising, he finds himself stripped of his power and forced to live an ordinary life in New York City. With his over-the-top personality and unconventional antics, Aladeen hilariously navigates through cultural clashes, political absurdities, and unexpected encounters, providing a sharp critique of dictatorial regimes and challenging societal norms. This bold and irreverent comedy, starring Sacha Baron Cohen, delivers a mix of outrageous humor and thought-provoking commentary on power, identity, and freedom.

Cast: Sacha Baron Cohen, Anna Faris, Ben Kingsley, Jason Mantzoukas, Adeel Akhtar, Fred Armisen, Kevin Corrigan, Megan Fox, Bobby Lee, Chris Parnell, Jessica St. Clair
